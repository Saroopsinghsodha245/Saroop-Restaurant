Our Story

Saroop Restaurant started in 2015 with a vision to serve fresh, delicious, high-quality meals. Our chefs carefully craft each dish with love and expertise, creating unforgettable dining experiences.

Meet Our Head Chef

Chef Sanjeev Kapoor has over 20 years of experience in Italian and French cuisine. He has worked in Michelin-starred restaurants and now brings his expertise to Saroop Restaurant.
